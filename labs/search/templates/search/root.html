Welcome to JudaicaLink Labs!

JudaicaLink Labs is a brand new portal where we aim at making various apps available to scholars and interested users to search and interact with our data.

Apps

For now, we provide the following apps:

  • JudaicaLink Search: Full-text search on our knowledge graph, e.g. the data we extract from sources like online encyclopedia.
  • Compact Memory Search: Full-text search on Compact Memory, a database of newspaper and journal articles from the 19th century.
